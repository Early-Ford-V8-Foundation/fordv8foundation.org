Did you know the Early Ford V-8 Museum has a YouTube Channel? 

If you missed our Sunday Talk last March featuring Ed Creighton and his Model B automobile, "Molly B", you can watch the whole presentation on our YouTube Channel! 

Ed is very knowledgeable about the Ford Model B's, which were a short-term production vehicle for Ford in 1932, as the Flathead V-8 engine was getting ready to debut. If you are a Ford history buff or a fan of obscure automotive history, you will enjoy learning many little-known facts about the Ford Model B! 

If you are an Early Ford V-8 Foundation member, you may have seen the article with many photos of the presentation and Molly B.  

Look for future Sunday Talks happening at the museum on Sunday afternoons! These are free with museum admission price. The next one will feature speaker David Sprowl, sharing the history of the partnership between the Lincoln Motor Company and Ford Motor Company! This will be a great one to attend, as there will be a Sunday Cruise-in that same afternoon, so you can enjoy the museum, wander the cars at the cruise-in, then come in and learn about Lincolns and Ford! All on Sunday, June 14th!
