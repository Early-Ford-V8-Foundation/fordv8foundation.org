Drive your favorite automobile out to our Sunday Cruise-ins! Every month will feature a different fun activity, that you can participate in, or just visit with the other fun car people! Check out the museum and J. Windle Event Center while you are here! 

Consider becoming a member of the EFV8 Foundation for only $25 a year - free museum entrance, news magazine, free swap meet space are just some of the benefits! If you love cars, be a part of preserving the classic car legacy for future generations! We have many ways you can get involved!
