Pat and Candice Fenner's beautiful 1951 Victoria was chosen for the People's Choice Award at the first annual Flatheads Forever Reunion, Saturday, June 27 on the Early Ford V-8 Museum campus. The award was a metal Flathead clock.  There were 50 fantastic flathead automobiles of various makes on the lot during the 9-5 event, making for a unique and fun car show! Check out our Facebook page for photos of nearly every vehicle! 

Mike Holton won the 50/50 drawing. In the silent auction John Smith's bid won the neon clock, Mark Campbell's bid won the Ford glass plate signed by Edsel Ford II.
