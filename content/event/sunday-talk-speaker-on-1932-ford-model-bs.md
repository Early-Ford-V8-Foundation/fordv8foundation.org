Ed and Kathy are bringing their beloved 1932 Model B "Molly" up for you to get a closer look at this rare car. Very few of this body style exist today because only 1200 were made, and they were the #1 car to be hot-rodded.  She has the original engine and is as close to original as you will ever see! She has been a frequent participant at The Henry Ford Museum events and won quite a few awards! Ed and Kathy have given educational talks to many crowds over the years, helping car enthusiasts understand the importance of the Model B's in Ford history. 

Admission is free with regular admission. Come early and tour the beautiful museum from 11-1:00 before settling down to an interesting show and tell with this gorgeous and rare green car!
